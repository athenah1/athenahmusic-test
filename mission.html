Why this page exists

This page is a promise that the essentials stay visible, verifiable, and easy to save.

Principles

  • Open access. The core info (music, shows, story, press kit) is readable without walls or gimmicks.
  • Clarity over clutter. Pages load as plain HTML + CSS. If scripts fail, the words still show.
  • Traceable updates. Every evergreen page includes a visible “Last updated” date.
  • Portable by design. You can print or save the essentials as clean PDFs.
  • Findable. A plain /start page and a simple navigation keep the key links in one place.
  • Accountability. When something changes, the /status page notes it.
  • Backed up. Critical pages are mirrored and archived so they don’t vanish.
